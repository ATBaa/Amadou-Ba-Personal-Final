Hobbies

I have a variety of hobbies that include:

  • Basketball
  • Soccer
  • Working out

Basketball

I have always enjoyed basketball both watching and playing it. The satisfaction of seeing a shot go in is unparalleled. I also like it because I can feel myself improving a lot.

Soccer

I like soccer for the same reasons I enjoy basketball; however, unlike basketball I watch it more than I play it. There’s just a sort of magic to it that no other pastime gives you — especially supporting FC Barcelona.

Working Out

This hobby is one I discovered very recently. I love to exercise because I can see the improvement I make, which makes me feel accomplished. Learning about different lifts and workouts also keeps me motivated.
